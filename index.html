Features

Everything in one crisp place

Built natively for iOS with the design quality you'd expect from a first-party Apple app.

⏰
Smart reminders
Set up to 5 alerts per document — 90, 60, 30, 14, 7, and 1 day out. Rich notifications deep-link straight to the record.
📋
Every document type
Passports, visas, driver's licenses, insurance policies, credit card fees, subscriptions, and custom entries.
📸
OCR scan & auto-fill
Point your camera at a document. DocuTimer reads the expiration date and fills it in automatically using Apple's Vision framework.
☁️
iCloud sync
Your data stays in sync across iPhone and iPad, encrypted end-to-end. No third-party servers ever touch your documents.
👨‍👩‍👧
Family profiles
Track documents for your whole household under one app. Color-coded profiles for each family member.
🔒
Face ID & privacy first
App lock with Face ID or Touch ID. All data lives on your device. No account required to get started.
